Research Projects

  • Data-Driven Turbulence Modeling: Using unsupervised machine learning and GEP to improve RANS closure models. (Current at University of Melbourne)
  • High-Speed Non-Equilibrium Flows: Development of numerical methods for viscous reactive flows in state-to-state and multi-temperature formulations. (SPbU)
  • UnDiFi-2D: Development of an unstructured discontinuity fitting code for 2D grids.
  • KAPPA Library: Kinetic Approach to Physical Processes in Atmospheres library in C++.
  • Viscous Fingering: Numerical investigation of viscous finger phenomenon in porous media. (SPbU & Gazprom Neft)
